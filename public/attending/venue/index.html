Venue

ICAPS 2023 will take place at the Charles University in Prague, Czech Republic. Prague is a political, cultural, and economic hub of central Europe, with a rich history and Romanesque, Gothic, Renaissance and Baroque architectures. It was the capital of the Kingdom of Bohemia and residence of several Holy Roman Emperors, most notably Charles IV (r. 1346–1378). Wikipedia

Doctoral Consortium Venue

Doctoral Consortium, ICAPS Council Meeting, and IWPSS will be held at Malá Strana Campus of Faculty of Mathematics and Physis (building "S") situated in the center of Lesser Town, a historic quarter under the Prague Castle. This is a building of the School of Computer Science, Faculty of Mathematics and Physics, Charles University. Web page with some interesting historical information (the building is from the 17th century) and virtual tour is available.

Address: Malostranské náměstí 2/25, 118 00 Praha 1, Czech Republic

Transport: The best way to get there is via public transport. The closest Metro station (line A) is Malostranská, from which you can either walk (10 minutes) or take a tram (one stop, lines 12, 20, 22) to station Malostranské náměstí, which is right in front of the faculty building.

For more details on traveling to and within Prague, look at page Travel Information.

Workshop and Main Conference Venue

ICAPS Workshops and Main Conference program will be held at Trója Campus of Faculty of Mathematics and Physis (building IMPAKT "N") situated next to the Vltava River. This is a new building of the School of Computer Science, Faculty of Mathematics and Physics, Charles University. Web page with some detailed information is available.

Address: V Holešovičkách 747/2, 180 00 Praha 8, Czech Republic

Transport: The best way to get there is via public transport. The closest Metro station (line C) is Nádraží Holešovice, from which you can either walk (20 minutes beside a highway) or take a bus #201 to stop Kuchyňka (first stop after crossing the river), which is very close to the faculty building.

For more details on traveling to and within Prague, look at page Travel Information.
